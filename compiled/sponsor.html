Sponsor Us

Hacks for Snacks does multiple events a year with kids ages 8-18. We jump start kids programming & electronics interest by introducing them to the exciting world of robots! We believe that the tactile, real world experience of progamming robots makes all the difference in generating excitement and interest in a child.

We need a few sponsors to help us get off the ground by donating resources to host these events. Namely we need hardware of the following sort.

  • SnapCircuits
  • Lego MindStorms
  • Sphero
  • Wearable Technology - Jewelry and Clothes
  • ARDrone
  • Arduino

Or cash to purchase the above hardware. Cash is also useful to make the events more enjoyable with snacks, drinks and prizes.

All payment for each event comes in the form of resources for the local food shelter. If you are interested in sponsoring us please reach out to info@hacksforsnacks.com.
